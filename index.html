Drug Addiction

by Dhanush GowdaNov 23rd,2020

Drug addiction has become a universal problem, especially in the teenagers. Several young individuals become dependent on different types constituents and stimulating medicines that comes hand-in-hand with narcotic outcome. It really affects them in numerous ways as they can get out of contact from their family and friends. The effect will be so severe, it will give them a thought of spending money on drugs without any limit and also arouses them to get the money in illegal ways. There will be some major effect on the individual’s health too.

There are different types of drugs such as – cocaine, meth, marijuana, crack, heroin etc which are usually supplied by drug peddlers. Heroin is one of the risky drugs that suppress your heart’s functions and henceforth achieve the narcotic effect. The drug consumption not only affects you but also the society which will be like drug abuse among youngsters. It is very important to stop drug addiction and maintain the physiological effects.

Millions of people in our country are suffering from drug addiction and if it goes in the same rate, the number may increase in the coming years. The common signs and symptoms of drug addiction are obsession with a particular substance, not interested to do the activities which you used to enjoy, etc. it may even include over usage of any drug. As the time passes, it will really affect our body and develop symptoms like fatigue, trembling, depression, anxiety, chills and sweating, behavioural changes, deprived coordination problems, etc.

As there is saying, prevention is always better than cure, it is always a good option to prevent people from drug abuse. We may not be able to prevent everyone from using drugs but there are few things we can do to avoid drug addiction like dealing with the peer pressure, treating emotional illness, inculcating healthy habits, etc.

Drug addiction is a complex illness that results from numerous factors like genetic predisposition, history of violence at home and stress. Understanding the main cause of drug addiction is one of the best ways to enhance the treatment options and results of drug addiction in upcoming years. I would like to conclude by saying, it is the responsibility of every citizen to act relevantly.
